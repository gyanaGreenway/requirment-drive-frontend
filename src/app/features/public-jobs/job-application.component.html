FAQs -

Why is my resume rejected?

We check each resume against the job description and company's preference (education, domain, previous experience, etc). If your resume is rejected then it's because there is a must have skill/criteria missing in your profile.

Was my resume even screened? I got rejected in less than 10 seconds.

We use state of the art OCR tech and fine-tuned AI models that are both fast and accurate. We have built Fabric so that candidates get immediate response - whether positive or negative. A negative response might sting, but it's better than getting no or a late response.

Can I reapply after making some changes in my resume?

We only accept one submission per job by any candidate.
